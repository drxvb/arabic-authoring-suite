# Saudi Tech Sector Q1 2026 Growth

## Claims to support

- Saudi tech sector grew 15% year-over-year in Q1 2026.
- Foreign direct investment in technology rose 30% versus Q1 2025.
- Cloud computing adoption hit 65% of large enterprises (up from 48% in 2024).
- Artificial intelligence projects in government doubled from 2024 baseline.
- Riyadh became the regional headquarters for 12 new multinational tech firms.
