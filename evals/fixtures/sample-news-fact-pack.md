# Riyadh International Tech Expo opens 5th edition

## Sources

[1] Saudi Press Agency wire, May 28 2026. https://www.spa.gov.sa/example-wire

Who: Saudi Minister of Communications and IT, Eng. Abdullah Al-Swaha.
What: Opening of the Riyadh International Tech Expo (RITE 2026).
When: Thursday May 28, 2026.
Where: Riyadh Front Exhibition Center.
Why: Annual showcase to position Riyadh as the regional tech hub aligned with Vision 2030.
How: 4-day programme covering AI, cloud computing, 5G, cybersecurity, and venture capital matchmaking.

Key figures:
- 800+ exhibiting companies from 45 countries
- 60,000+ expected attendees
- USD 12 billion in announced investments
- 12 new multinational tech firms confirming Riyadh as regional headquarters
- 200+ government technology contracts to be signed during the expo
