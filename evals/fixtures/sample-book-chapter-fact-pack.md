## Foundational phase (2016-2020) claims

- Vision 2030 launched April 25, 2016 with explicit "Digital Economy" pillar.
- National Digital Transformation Unit established 2017.
- Saudi Data and AI Authority (SDAIA) created 2019.
- National AI Strategy launched 2020.

## Pandemic acceleration (2020-2022) claims

- Tawakkalna contact-tracing app reached 30+ million users by Q2 2021.
- Sehhaty health platform processed 200+ million digital services.
- Distance learning Madrasati platform scaled to serve 7 million students.
- Cloud computing adoption jumped from 28% to 56% across government agencies.
- Cybersecurity National Authority issued emergency directives extending digital trust frameworks.
